Our History

  1. 2022

    Start of the Fire...

    Stage Fright breakthrough came when they played at a local music festival in early 2022. The crowd was electrified, and a small but devoted fanbase began to take shape. In June 2022 Stage Fright joins Indie record label TNR Records. Over a few months, the band played at bars and spread the word about their upcoming debut album with the label. Stage Fright's " Prometheus" sold 13k copies in the first week. Stage Fright released Music Videos for the Prometheus album.

  2. 2023

    The Seeds of Growth...

    Stage Fright announces that their sophomore album will be released in July 2023. When Stage Fright dropped their sophomore album, Acantha, It sold over 20k copies in the first week, Beating their freshman album by 7k copies. Stage Fright releases Music Videos for the Acantha album in mid-July. Stage Fright partners with a clothing brand and drops the first waves of merchandise for their two albums, Prometheus and Acantha.

  3. 2024

    The Ourea...

    Stage Fright announces their junior album "Nyx dropping in April 2024. Stage Fright drops the Nyx album along with Nyx album merch. It sold well over 35k copies in the first week beating their first two albums combined. Stage Fright announces that TNR Records and the band have organized a tour and a new album to be started and released in 2025.

  4. 2025

    Forged by Fire and Speed...

    Hermes Tour starts and Hephaestus album releases.
